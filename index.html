Understanding the SD Request Portal and PAT Workflow.

Service Delivery is the backbone of FiberOne operations. While we are known for external service through FST and Technology Support, SD is now expanding into internal Infrastructure Development -- building the software tools that empower our own teams to work more efficiently.

Exactly. We've realized that to provide world-class service to customers, our internal systems must be just as world-class. The new Infrastructure branch is dedicated to "serving the server" -- creating apps and automations specifically for FiberOne staff.

PAT (Provisional Acceptability Test) is our structured workflow for validating infrastructure projects. It flows through MEC, Project Team, Planning Team, SD Team, and Final MEC Review. Each stage has specific responsibilities and checkpoints to ensure quality.

By developing better internal tools, our FST and Technology Support teams can diagnose issues faster, access better data in the field, and reduce repetitive manual tasks, allowing them to focus on high-quality service delivery.

Yes. Our commitment to external customers remains unchanged. This internal development system is an addition to our core mission, ensuring our internal infrastructure is as robust as the fiber network we deploy.
